Fork Knives and Glasses is our TriBeCa neighborhood taverna serving
Chef Andrew Carmellini's soul-satisfying urban Italian menu.
Our bustling corner restaurant has been welcoming New Yorkers morning,
noon and night since 2009 with a warm and comfortable atmosphere,
leisurely Italian spirit and heartfelt cooking.

WHAT DOES LOCANDA VERDE MEAN?
'Locanda' in Italian is a local place to eat, drink and maybe stay the night. Directly translated, Locanda Verde means "green inn."


WHO IS THE CHEF?
Andrew Carmellini is the chef and co-owner. You can read more about him here: WWW.ANDREWCARMELLINI.COM. The Chef della Cucina is Damian Franqui.


WHO IS THE PASTRY CHEF?
Kierin Baldwin


WHAT KIND OF FOOD DO YOU SERVE?
We serve a seasonal, urban Italian menu of Antipasti, house made Pastas and Secondi.


WHAT ABOUT COOKBOOKS?
Chef Carmellini has written ‘Urban Italian’ (Bloomsbury, 2009) and ‘American Flavor’ (EccoPress, 2011). Both are available for sale at the restaurant.


WHO OWNS THE RESTAURANT?
Chef Carmellini, Josh Pickard, Luke Ostrom and Ken Friedman with their partners at The Greenwich Hotel, Ira Drukier, Richard Born & Robert De Niro.


WHO DESIGNED THE RESTAURANT?
Ken Friedman with Meyer Davis Studio.


IS THERE A DRESS CODE?
No formalities, come as you are.


IS THERE CORKAGE FEE?
We have a large Italian wine list that spans all regions with the addition of a reserve list of fine and rare wines. If you would like to bring your own wine we ask that you pay a corkage fee of $35 per bottle, and bring no more than two 750 ml bottles.
